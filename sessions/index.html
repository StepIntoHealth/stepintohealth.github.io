Sessions

You will be asked to fill out a questionnaire, which is private and cofidential, to bring with you to the first session. This gives me a chance to look at your medical history, your symptoms and to see what your diet and lifestyle is like. In the assessment session your arm muscles are tested using Kinesiology as you lie on a couch, the approach is gentle, safe and not intrusive and the results are known at once.

Consultation and assessment - First session

I will

  • take a case history whilst we talk through your questionnaire.
  • show you a simple relaxation technique before testing, where this is agreed and appropriate.
  • test your body’s response to a wide range of foods and chemicals that may be the cause of your ailment; what you eat, any products you may be using and any natural or artificial chemicals you may come into contact with in your immediate environment.
  • test for Candida Albicans and where appropriate give dietary advice on how to reduce the level of Candida Albicans.
  • give advice on how to look out for and avoid inappropriate foods. We'll talk through recipes and how to plan meals. You'll be amazed to see how easy and enjoyable it is to 'cook without'.

Cost £50.00 for the session. This session takes approximately 2 hours.

Follow up session

What happens in this session will depend on the test results of the first session, however in most cases

I will

  • check the level of Candida Albicans and calculate it's reduction
  • test for any deficiencies in vitamins and minerals.
  • give nutritional advice on how to add vitamins and minerals, through food, into your diet.
  • talk through simple relaxtion techniques and when appropriate look at Dr Bach's Flower Remedies

Cost £35.00 for this session. This session takes approximately 1½ hours.
